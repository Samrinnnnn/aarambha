Grooming tips

  • 01Good Underwear

    Along with wearing new underwear, you need also make the appropriate fabric selections. When in doubt, avoid synthetic fabrics and stick with basic cotton. Lightweight materials keep the area dry and minimize sweating.

  • 02Wash Up After

    This advice on good intimate hygiene applies to both you and your spouse. Neglecting post-intimacy cleanup might result in painful infections.

  • 03Use Gentle Intimate Washes

    Regular gentle washing of sensitive areas is necessary. The typical male personal care items could be a tad too abrasive for intimate areas. Invest in a quality intimate wash for guys to prevent any sensitivity-related problems.

  • 04Hydration

    Keep yourself properly hydrated at all times. According to certain studies, erectile dysfunction and dehydration go hand in hand. Water is essential for the healthy operation of many of your body systems, including those involved in reproduction.
